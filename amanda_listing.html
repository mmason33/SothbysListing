Area Description

Located in the heart of the Sierra Nevada Foothills, only a short drive in either direction to Sacramento or Lake Tahoe, this property is located in the center of Amador County which is quickly becoming an area known for beautiful scenery, excellent wine and its rich historic experience. Amador County bills itself as "The Heart of the Mother Lode" and lies within the Gold Country. There is a substantial wine-growing industry in the county. Along with Napa Valley AVA and Sonoma Valley AVA wine growing regions of California, the Shenandoah Valley has become a thriving and popular wine growing region of California. Amador County's nearly 10,000-foot rise in elevation from west to east makes it an ideal place for year-round recreation. Cycle through golden rolling hills, ski or snowboard on the slopes of Kirkwood, fish in rivers and lakes, or play a round of golf. Plenty of hikes for all abilities and fishing spots galore! Stroll through the charming streets of the historic towns and enjoy restaurants that rival some of the best dining in California, from award winning bakeries & pubs, to fine dining. The unique charm will draw you in…and the people will make you want to stay.
